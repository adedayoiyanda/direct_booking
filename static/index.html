Help Centre

Frequently Asked
Questions

How do I book a property?+
Click any property to open its full details. Choose your dates, verify availability, then click "Book Now". You'll complete payment securely via Paystack.
How do I check if my dates are available?+
Every property detail page shows an availability calendar with booked dates highlighted in red. You can also enter your dates directly in the booking card to get an instant availability check.
What payment methods are accepted?+
We accept all major debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), bank transfers, and USSD payments — all powered securely by Paystack.
What happens after I pay?+
You receive an immediate confirmation email with stay details and check-in instructions. Your booking status updates to "Confirmed" once payment clears.
Can I cancel or modify my booking?+
Cancellation policies vary by property. Please contact us directly and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.
I'm a property owner — how do I list?+
Visit the Landlords section in the menu and fill out the enquiry form. Our team will contact you within 24 hours.
Do you offer corporate accommodation?+
Yes! Visit the Corporate section for team stays, executive housing, and project-based accommodation with flexible billing options.
