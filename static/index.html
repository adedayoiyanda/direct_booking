Help Centre

Frequently Asked
Questions

How do I book a property?+
Click any property to view its full details. Choose your dates, select the number of adults and children, then click "Request to Book". Fill in your contact details and we'll get back to you within a few hours to confirm your stay.
How does the request-to-book process work?+
Once you submit a booking request, our team receives an email notification with your details and the property link. We'll review availability, confirm your booking, and send you payment instructions — all within a few hours.
How do I check if my dates are available?+
Every property detail page shows an availability calendar with booked dates highlighted in red. You can also enter your dates directly in the booking card to get an instant availability check before you submit a request.
What payment methods are accepted?+
After your booking request is confirmed, we accept bank transfers, card payments, and mobile money. Our team will send you full payment instructions.
Can I cancel or modify my booking?+
Cancellation policies vary by property. Please contact us directly and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.
I'm a property owner — how do I list?+
Visit the Landlords section in the menu and fill out the enquiry form. Our team will contact you within 24 hours.
Do you offer corporate accommodation?+
Yes! Visit the Corporate section for team stays, executive housing, and project-based accommodation with flexible billing options.
